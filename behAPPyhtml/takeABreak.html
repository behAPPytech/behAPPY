Take a break!


Feeling Anxious?

  • Meditate
  • Do yoga
  • Try deep breathing
  • Listen to calming music
  • Take warm shower


    • Feeling Down?

  • Listening to happy music
  • Try deep breathing
  • Making a list of everything that makes you happy
  • Call a friend


    • Feeling Tired?

  • Take a 20 minute or less power nap (set a timer!)
  • Listen to upbeat music
  • Stretch
  • Eat a heathy snack


    • Just Feeling Bored of Work?

  • Take a walk
  • Doodle
  • Write a creative story
  • Organize your bedroom
